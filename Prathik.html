What is special about Bangalore?

Bangalore is sometimes referred to as the “Silicon Valley of India”
(or “IT capital of India”) because of its role as the nation's leading
information technology (IT) exporter. Indian technological organisations
ISRO, Infosys, Wipro and HAL are headquartered in the city.

Yellahanka Is Like Capital Of Banglore And Its Famous For Traditional Weavers

Yelahanka is a traditional place of weavers. The silk handloom has been
the lifeline of Yelahanka people for over 2 centuries, and even now in areas like
Kamakshamma layout, Agrahara layout, Kogilu, Maruti nagar, and Chowdeshwari
layout silk saree development can be seen.
